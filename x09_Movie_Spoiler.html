Movie Quote

Plot

Underworld follows the vampire Selene, a deathdealer, as she fights in a war that has been raging for centuries. A war between vampires and lycans, better known as werewovles. She discovers a plot to undermine the vampire elders and discovers a treachery that has been going on for as long as the war.
